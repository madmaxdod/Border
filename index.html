How it works:

  • The camera detects people in the frame
  • When detected, it focuses on the lower body (shoes/feet area)
  • As you move closer to the camera, the image becomes smaller
  • As you move away, the image becomes larger
  • This creates an inverse relationship between distance and visual scale
